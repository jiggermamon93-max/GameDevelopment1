HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

🚀Dodge asteroids, shoot enemies, survive as long as possible and reach the highest level!

🕹️ CONTROLS

◀▶Press LEFT / RIGHT buttons to move.
🔥Press FIRE to shoot. Hold for auto-fire!
⏸Top-right to pause.

🔫 WEAPONS PER LEVEL

1️⃣Single Shot – Fast single bullet
2️⃣Double Shot – Two side-by-side bullets
3️⃣Spread Shot – 3 bullets in a fan
4️⃣Laser Beam – Continuous laser!
5️⃣Plasma Burst – Big slow plasma ball (3 dmg)
6️⃣+HYPER CANNON – Everything combined!

📊 SCORING

🌑Asteroid = +10 pts  |  Enemy = +25 pts
📈Every 250 pts = next level + new weapon!
💥Hit = lose 1 life. You have 3 lives.
