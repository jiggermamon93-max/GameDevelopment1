HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

🚀Pilot your ship! Dodge asteroids and shoot enemies. Survive as long as possible!

🕹️ CONTROLS

👆Drag the bottom area left/right to move your ship.
🔥Tap the FIRE button (bottom-right) to shoot.
⏸Tap pause (top-right) to pause.

📊 SCORING

🌑Asteroid destroyed = +10 pts
👾Enemy destroyed = +25 pts
📈Every 200 pts = next level (faster!)
💥Hit = lose 1 life. You have 3 lives.

✨ POWER-UPS (catch them!)

⚡Speed Boost – Move faster 5 sec
🛡️Shield – Invincible 4 sec
💣Multi-Shot – Triple bullets 6 sec
❤️Extra Life – +1 life (max 3)
