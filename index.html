HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

🚀Pilot your ship, dodge asteroids, and shoot enemies. Survive as long as possible and reach the highest score!

🕹️ CONTROLS

👆Drag anywhere on screen to move your ship left and right.
🔥Tap the FIRE button (bottom-right) to shoot bullets.
⏸Tap the pause icon (top-right) to pause.

⚠️ RULES

💥Hitting an asteroid costs 1 life. You have 3 lives.
🌠Shoot asteroids for +10 points each.
👾Shoot enemy ships for +25 points.
📈Every 200 points = new level. Speed increases!
