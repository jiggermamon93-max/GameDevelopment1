HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

🚀Dodge asteroids & enemy fire. Shoot enemies. Survive to reach the highest level!

🕹️ CONTROLS

◀▶Press LEFT / RIGHT buttons to move your ship.
🔥Press FIRE to shoot. Hold for auto-fire!
⏸Top-right button to pause.

🔫 WEAPONS BY LEVEL

1️⃣Single Shot – Fast single bullet
2️⃣Double Shot – Two bullets side by side
3️⃣Spread Shot – 3 bullets in a fan
4️⃣Laser Beam – Continuous laser!
5️⃣Plasma Burst – Huge slow plasma ball
6️⃣+HYPER CANNON – All weapons combined!

📊 SCORING

🌑Asteroid = +10 pts  |  Enemy = +25 pts
📈Every 250 pts = next level + new weapon!
💥Hit = lose 1 life. You start with 3 lives.
